**Camdrop** is the best and most intuitive Dropcam client for Windows and Windows Phone. Seamlessly drop in on your home, kids, or mischievous pets from anywhere in the world in beautiful HD. While a Dropcam account is required to use this application, you don't have to own a Dropcam. You can browse the directory of public camera feeds and view them with Camdrop. Enjoy peace of mind knowing that you can stay connected to your Dropcam and the things you care about most right from your phone or tablet using Camdrop.
